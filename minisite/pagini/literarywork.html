Literary Work

Besides his parents he published several dozen scientific disputes, artisles, reviews and a 396-page autobiography under the name Michael Pupin, From Immigrant to Inventor [Scribner's, 1923]. He won the annual Pulitzer Prize for Biography or Autobiography. It was published in Serbian in 1929 under the title From pastures to scientist [Od pasnjaka do naucenjaka].
